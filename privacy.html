Privacy Policy - Zero Cap Finance Analytics Tool

This application accesses only the authenticated operator's own TikTok account data (profile information, follower counts, video statistics).

Data accessed:

  • Profile fields the operator has authorized via OAuth scopes user.info.basic, user.info.profile, user.info.stats.
  • The operator's own video metadata via video.list.

Data is stored locally on the operator's device only. No data is shared with third parties, transmitted to remote servers, or used for advertising.

The operator may revoke access at any time via TikTok account settings.
